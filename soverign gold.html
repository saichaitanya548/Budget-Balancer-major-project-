Sovereign Gold Investment

What is Sovereign Gold?

Sovereign Gold Bonds (SGBs) are government securities denominated in grams of gold. They are substitutes for holding physical gold. Investors have to pay the issue price in cash, and the bonds will be redeemed in cash on maturity.

Benefits of Investing in Sovereign Gold Bonds

Sovereign Gold Bonds provide several benefits:

  • Interest: SGBs offer an annual interest rate of 2.5% payable semi-annually.
  • Safety: Since these bonds are issued by the government, they are considered safe investments.
  • No Storage Issues: Unlike physical gold, SGBs do not require storage and come with no risk of theft.
  • Tax Benefits: Capital gains tax arising on redemption of SGB to an individual is exempted.
